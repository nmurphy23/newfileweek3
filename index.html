Welcome to the comparison of two luxury cars models, the Mercedes-AMG GT 63SE and the Porsche 911 GT3 RS.

Mercedes-AMG GT 63SE: It's a hybrid, just like AMG's racecards

Developed by racing champions to be one, the most advanced AMG GT Coupe yet pairs a handcrafted biturbo V8 with an electric rear motor, to send over 800 hp and 1,000 lb-ft of torque to all four wheels.

Porsche 911 GT3 RS: Maximum performance.

As soon as the lights give the starting signal, the new 911 GT3 RS delivers unbelievable performance of up to 342 lb-ft on the racetrack. With up to 518 hp, taking you from 0-60 mph in 3.0 seconds, it can reach a top track speed of 184 mph. The lightweight stainless steel sport exhaust system ensures an unfiltered sound experience up to a maximum of 9,000 rpm.
